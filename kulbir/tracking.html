Dynamic Visiblity                



Delivery reliability plays a crucial role in improving customer service. Demurrage
and detention are critical cost items and can become a huge issue for shippers.
Live Tracking provides the shipment visibility needed to take corrective action in
case of logistical problems and inform their customers about any delays.

In the past, having real-time shipment visibility into load location, status and
dynamic ETAs across a supply chain was simply not possible.Goods Carrier
provides shippers and carriers with much needed visibility into the
locations of vehicles for optimum control. This lets them quickly react and
mitigate unexpected events such as traffic jams and incidents.

Goods Carrier provides total supply chain visibility on all ongoing
transportation operations and immediate notifications whenever a shipment gets
delayed. This greatly reduces the number of failed deliveries and substantially
improves customer satisfaction in a cost effective way.
